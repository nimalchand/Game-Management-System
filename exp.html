About The Game

Nexus Wars is a groundbreaking space strategy RPG that combines intense real-time combat with deep empire building. Set in the year 3157, you play as a fledgling commander rising through the ranks of the Galactic Federation. Your choices will determine the fate of the galaxy.

Vast Universe

Explore over 200 unique star systems with diverse planets, space stations, and anomalies.

Deep Faction System

Align with one of 5 major factions or remain independent - each with unique storylines.

Ship Customization

Design and build your perfect fleet from hundreds of modular components.

Game Features

Tactical Combat

Real-time with pause combat system that rewards strategic positioning and fleet composition.

Dynamic Economy

A living galaxy where prices fluctuate based on faction conflicts and player actions.

Character Progression

Develop your commander with a flexible skill tree and reputation system.
